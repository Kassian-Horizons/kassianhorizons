Aeloria's: Last Hope

The townspeople never stopped talking about this hero, praising his bravery and unwavering determination to protect the innocent. They believed he could conquer any obstacle in his path. Eryndor, the hero of Aeloria, had long passed away after defeating his arch-nemesis Kaelvorr Shadowborn. For many years, peace reigned in the land. But now a new war has started, and the people are desperate for their hero to return. A group of young individuals stumbled upon a legend that claimed the only way to bring Eryndor back was to find the elusive Soulforge Crystal. Will their quest be successful in reviving the hero, or will it unleash something even more dangerous?
